PRIVACY POLICY

DOCUMENT REF: POL-PRIV-2026

Pyronite respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website.

Last updated: January 10, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name, email address, phone number, company name
  • Information submitted via contact or inquiry forms
  • Technical data such as IP address, browser type, and device details
  • Website usage data and interaction metrics

2. Purpose of Data Collection

We use collected data to:

  • Respond to inquiries and business requests
  • Communicate about services and engagements
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Maintain security and prevent misuse
  • Meet legal or regulatory obligations

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Pyronite does not sell or trade personal data. Data may be shared only with:

  • Secure infrastructure and hosting environments
  • Controlled access to data
  • Periodic security reviews and best practices

4. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill its intended purpose or comply with legal requirements.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures including:

  • Secure infrastructure and hosting environments
  • Controlled access to data
  • Periodic security reviews and best practices

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion
  • Withdraw consent for communications
  • Object to certain processing activities

Requests can be sent to: info@pyronite.com

7. Cookies & Tracking

Pyronite uses cookies and similar technologies to analyze website traffic and enhance user experience. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

8. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.
